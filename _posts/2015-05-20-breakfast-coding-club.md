Evie started up a breakfast coding club in the Haskell for Cats meetup group and extended it to the N-languages meetup group.

I've joined in a few breakfast coding club and it has been very good. Just getting an hour or two before work has really improved my productivity and increased my focus.

Today, I spent my time working on getting this site up and making this post. Evie spent time trying to dig herself out of Cabal hell (and maybe taking poor Mark with her.) Kat was totally in the zone on something for work.
